Bra Size Calculator in Inches & cm

Our bra size calculator allows you to easily switch between inches (used for US and UK sizes) and centimeters (used for EU, AU, and NZ sizes). Select your preferred measurement unit, enter your underbust and overbust measurements, and the calculator will instantly display accurate results for both band and cup size. Whether you’re shopping locally or online, you can measure in inches or centimeters — all in one place.

Underbust: Wrap the measuring tape snugly around your ribcage, directly under your bust, keeping it level. This is your band size.

Overbust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust (usually over the nipples), keeping the tape parallel to the floor and not too tight.
